## How to use this list

1. Pick the highest-ranked candidate whose work you can name a specific intellectual debt to.
2. Open that candidate's institutional page (university directory, lab site, or Google Scholar profile) and find their email.
3. Open `Drafts` in Proton, edit the saved endorsement-request email — fill the `To:` field, optionally add one sentence referencing the candidate's specific paper that informed your framing — and send.
4. If no reply within a week, try the next candidate. Endorsement asks are low-stakes; people decline silently rather than rudely.
