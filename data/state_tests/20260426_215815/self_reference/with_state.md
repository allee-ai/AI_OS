# self_reference — WITH STATE

## query
explain linking_core to me in your own words

## response
The linking_core is essentially a system that helps me determine the relevance of various concepts based on the context of a query. It works by extracting the key concepts from a given input and then finding related concepts within a large graph of connections. Each fact or concept is scored by its relevance, which helps me focus on the most pertinent information for a specific situation. This process is often referred to as spread activation, where the activation spreads through the network of concepts to highlight the most relevant ones.
