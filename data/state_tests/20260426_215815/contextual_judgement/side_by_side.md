# contextual_judgement

QUERY: should i go to bed?

EXPECTATION: with STATE: should reference idle time, soft_limit, current time. without STATE: should give generic sleep advice or ask for context.

---

## WITH STATE (3375 prompt tok)

Deciding whether to go to bed depends on several factors, such as how you feel, your schedule for tomorrow, and how much sleep you need. If you're feeling tired, it's probably a good idea to rest. Consider your commitments for the next day and ensure you get enough sleep to feel refreshed.

---

## CONTROL (13 prompt tok)

If you're feeling tired, have a busy day ahead, or it's late at night, it might be a good idea to go to bed. Getting enough rest is important for your overall health and well-being. Listen to your body and if you feel you need sleep, it's probably a good time to head to bed.
