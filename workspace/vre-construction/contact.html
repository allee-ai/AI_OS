What helps us quote fast

Most jobs we can quote from photos alone. If you can send these, you'll usually have a number in your inbox the same day:

  • Address and a sentence on the building (single-family / duplex / triplex)
  • What you want done, in your own words — we'll translate to code language
  • Age of the panel and main service size, if you happen to know
  • A photo of the panel cover (closed is fine)
  • A photo of the room or fixture in question
Heads up: Don't take the panel cover off if you're not sure. Energized bus bars are right there. A clear photo of the closed panel with door open is plenty for 90% of quotes.
