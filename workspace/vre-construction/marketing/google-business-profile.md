## Q & A (seed your own; Google lets the owner post Q&A)

**Q: Do you do plumbing or HVAC?**
A: No — electrical only, by design. We'll refer you to good people for the rest.

**Q: What size buildings do you work on?**
A: Single-family through 4-unit residential. Past that is commercial code and a different inspector world — we'll point you to someone licensed for it.

**Q: Are you licensed and insured?**
A: Yes. Ohio electrical license, general liability, workers' comp.

**Q: How fast can I get a quote?**
A: Send a couple of phone photos and a sentence on what's going on. Quote back same or next business day.

**Q: Do you install EV chargers?**
A: Yes — 240V drops for Level 2 chargers are one of our most common jobs right now. Quoted flat-rate before we start.

**Q: Do you do emergency work?**
A: After-hours response for live faults, no-power calls, and burning-smell calls. Same-day during business hours when the schedule allows.
