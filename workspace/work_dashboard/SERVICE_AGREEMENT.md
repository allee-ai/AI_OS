# Property Management Service Agreement

**Between:** Peak Properties ("Provider")  
**And:** Jake ____________ ("Owner")  
**Effective Date:** _______________

---

## 1. Retainer

Owner maintains a rolling retainer balance with Provider. An initial deposit of **$______** is due at signing.

When the retainer balance falls below **$______**, Owner agrees to replenish within **15 days** of written notice.

## 2. Billable Work

All work is billed against the retainer balance. Each task is classified as one of:

| Type | Billing |
|------|---------|
| **Flat** | Fixed price agreed before work begins |
| **Hourly** | Billed at **$____/hr**, tracked to the quarter hour |

All work requires Owner approval before execution, except emergency repairs under $500.

## 3. Work Authorization

- Tenant submits maintenance request via portal
- Provider reviews and estimates (flat price or hourly estimate)
- Provider submits for Owner approval via portal
- Owner approves or denies
- Provider performs work, logs completion, balance is deducted

## 4. Statements

Owner has real-time access to their portal showing:
- Current retainer balance
- All deposits and deductions
- Task history with type, description, and amount

## 5. Expenses

Materials and third-party vendor costs are passed through at cost, itemized separately. Purchases over $400 require prior Owner approval.

## 6. Term

This agreement is month-to-month. Either party may terminate with **30 days** written notice.
