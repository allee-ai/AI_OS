## What we do (residential, ≤3-family only)

- **Panel upgrades and service changes.** 100A → 200A swaps, mast replacements, meter base, grounding electrode systems.
- **Sub-panels and feeder runs.** Garages, additions, basement units, separate-meter conversions.
- **Breaker swaps, AFCI/GFCI updates.** Bringing aged panels up to current code where it matters.
- **New circuits and dedicated lines.** Window units, sump pumps, freezers, workshop tools.
- **240V drops.** EV chargers, dryers, ranges. Tenants asking for chargers — we install, you charge a rent premium.
- **Outlets, switches, dimmers, fixtures, fans.** All the small stuff between turnovers.
- **Whole-house surge protection.** $200-ish device that prevents $5K of lightning damage. Worth it on every roof.
- **Fault tracing and troubleshooting.** Dead circuits, GFCI nuisance trips, flickering lights, bad neutrals.
- **Knob-and-tube assessments.** Real call on whether to leave it, retire it, or rewire — written up so insurance accepts it.
- **Pre-purchase electrical walkthroughs.** Before you close on the property. Find the $8K rewire before you own it, not after.

## Why a 3-family-and-under landlord should call us first

- **Code matters more on rentals.** A panel that's "fine for a homeowner" can fail an insurance inspection, a Section 8 inspection, or a sale. We quote with that in mind.
- **Tenant complaints don't wait.** Flickering lights, dead outlets, breakers that won't reset — these turn into 1-star reviews fast. Quick diagnostics with a capped bill keeps them out of your inbox.
- **EV demand is here.** Tenants ask. A clean 240V drop in a parking spot is a competitive amenity that pays for itself in a year of rent premium.
- **Pre-purchase walkthroughs save deals.** We'll walk a property with you before close, write up what's there, and give you a number for what it'd take to make it right. Use it for your offer or your reno budget.
- **Repeat work is the relationship.** First job is the test. Investor pipelines are how we want to work.
